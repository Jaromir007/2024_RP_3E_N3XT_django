The Future of 3D Slicing

N3XT is a web-based slicer that allows you to slice your 3D models right from your browser. No need to download or install anything. Just upload your model and start slicing!

Slicing has never been so easy!

Open Slicer
Features

Slice directly from your browser

Downloading slicer app takes time - especially if you often switch between operation systems and devices. That's why we created N3XT - a great solution for everyone.

No login required

Yes, that's right - you don't have to login to use our slicer. Login is required only if you want to use features like saving your models or slicing preferences.

Easy to use

All you need is stable internet connection. If you still get lost, just head over to our tutorial and read the basics.

Free and open source

Our goal is to democratize 3D slicing. We developed a new slicer from scratch, focusing on simplicity and ease of use, making it accessible for everyone.
